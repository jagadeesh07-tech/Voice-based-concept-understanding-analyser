📐 Scoring Formula

Score = 0.60 × Ssem + 0.40 × Sflu
Sflu = 0.25·E + 0.30·P + 0.25·F + 0.20·R
ERMS Energy score
PPause quality score
FFiller word score
RSpeaking rate score
≥ 75%Strong Understanding
50–74%Moderate Understanding
< 50%Poor Understanding
